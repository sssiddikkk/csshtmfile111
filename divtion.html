Importance of learning English

English is spoken almost all over the world. So, it is called an international language. According to the world statistics, 350 million people spoken English as a first language and another 300 people use it as second language. It is the official or semiofficial language in more than 60 countries and of many international organization.

Learning English is important in all spheres of life. We cannot acquire higher education without having a good command over English, because all the books on higher education are written in English. International companies use English to communicate between offices in different countries. Nowadays more than 80% of all information in the world computer is in English. So we see that efficiency in English helps us to get good jobs and better salaries. We should use English. We should learn English.
